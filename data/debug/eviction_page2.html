Legal Disclaimer

In order to provide the public with immediate access to newly eFiled, non-confidential, civil complaints, these complaints will be made available through this page immediately upon receipt. Please be advised that these complaints have not been subjected to Summit County Clerk of Court’s quality assurance procedure. As soon as the review process has been completed and the complaint is found to be in compliance, it will be posted on the Clerk’s Public Access system as filed. Until that posting, the complaint should be used subject to this limitation. The Clerk is not responsible for any unapproved filing that is subsequently rejected.
